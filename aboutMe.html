Julia Jóźwiak

Born on January 1 1990 in Poznań.

Participant of the Polish Dance Theater projects.
A graduate of open ballet classes conducted by Ryszard Baranowski at the General Ballet School.
A former student of the School of Contemporary Dance in Poznań, founded by Natalie Draganik-Franke.

She studied with Michael Getman, a former dancer of Ohad Naharin, during the Format Zero workshop in Warsaw.

She qualified and took part in several performing arts projects such as: "Mimo chodem", at the "Pola Nireńska Dance School" at the Polin Museum in Warsaw, at the "Anti-Static" exhibition at the National Museum in Poznań, also at the opening of Short Waves Festival.

She perfected herself at numerous workshops of Polish and foreign choreographers, including during classes at Pineapple Dance Studio in London.

She taught contemporary dance and dance theater for adults. She founded and ran a children's dance theater Lirykal in Stęszew for several years.

She currently lives and creates in Wrocław under the supervision of Przemysław Błaszczak in the Grotowski Institute .
